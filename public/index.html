Northwest Plains District Family Camp

July 8th - 12th, 2009

Widewater Retreat & Ministry Center. Liberty Center, OH

Getting Up The Family

Family Camp is an annual 5 day church camp your family to bond. At Family Camp we offer a variety of structured progamming including horseback riding and recreational activities. You and your family will also have plenty of free time to explore and relax.
